AIO
ClearTV have produced AIO with cooperation with manufacturers, such as, Samsung, Philips, Skyworth and LG and other brand manufacturers. Brand TV as carrier, customized original TV chip software and running ClearTV Terminal Software on the chip, ClearTV AIO operate with high stability. Compared with traditional display and IMS STB, ClearTV AIO without STB has the characteristics of potable and cheap.

HUALUXE Hotel - InterContinental Hotels Group

In October 15th 2016, with all the commission work has done, our cooperation with InterContinental Hotels Group has made a perfect end.

The IPTV system we designed for HUA adopt the model of TV plus STB(dual-module). And Clear customized a remote device for HUA with the hotel logo as well. This system has been working very well till now.

Plateno Hotels Group

The Plateno Group is one of the most influential and innovative customer-centric company in China. The Plateno group has more than 20 brands with over 3,700 hotels in 300 destinations and 80 million loyalty members. Now, the company is expanding to Southeast Asia, Europe, Africa and other overseas markets.

Clear’s cooperation with Plateno Hotels Group starts from December 2015. In 2016 we have completed its sub-brand Lavande Hotel’s IPTV system and several other bands’ system construction is on the way.

Black Diamond Hotel

Lagos, Federal Republic of Nigeria

Clear had provide customized Hotel IPTV system for around three hundred hotel rooms for our client in 2009, this VOD system includes video stream media server and STB, which supports live and time-shift of satellite tv shows and thousands of VOD channels. This whole system was English and we specially customized scroll caption, room charge management and so on for this hotel.

In 2015, Clear made an upgrade for BDH and add two hundreds points for their new hotel Bayshore.
